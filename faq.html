Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I sign up?

Click “Sign Up,” fill your info, verify your email — then you’re ready.

2. Is there a free tier?

Yes! Our Starter plan lets you send up to 5 invoices monthly at ₦0.

3. Who is AutoCorp for?

Small businesses, consultants, and freelancers who need quick, easy invoicing.

4. Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely — switch plans anytime from your dashboard without losing data.

5. How does Auto Follow-Up work?

Set reminder schedules and AutoCorp emails or texts late payers automatically.

6. Can I export my invoice data?

Yes. Download CSV reports for all invoices and payments.

7. What payment methods are supported?

We integrate with Paystack and Flutterwave out of the box.

8. Is my data secure?

We use SSL/TLS encryption and industry-standard security practices.
