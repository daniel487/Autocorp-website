Our Story

AutoCorp was founded by Folashade “Princess” Akinbode—an inventor and serial entrepreneur frustrated by late payments and endless paperwork. In 2024 she rallied a team of engineers and designers to build a tool so simple, anyone could automate their billing in seconds.

Our Mission

Empower small business owners to focus on growth by automating invoicing, payment tracking, and reminders—with zero tech headaches.

Our Vision

A future where every entrepreneur, no matter their technical skill, gets paid on time, every time—fueling more thriving businesses across Africa and beyond.
