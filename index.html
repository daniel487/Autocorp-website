Our Story

Founded by Folashade “Princess” Akinbode, AutoCorp emerged from a personal frustration with the inefficiencies and delays plaguing everyday business operations—specifically late payments and the endless stream of manual paperwork. As a seasoned entrepreneur, Folashade knew firsthand how time-consuming and error-prone invoicing and payment follow-ups could be. Determined to create a better way, she envisioned a solution that would eliminate the need for spreadsheets, email reminders, and complicated software setups. In 2024.

Mission

Empower entrepreneurs to focus on growth by automating invoicing, tracking & reminders—no tech headaches.

Vision

A world where every small business gets paid on time, every time—fueling thriving economies across Africa and beyond.
